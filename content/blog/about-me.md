## 👋 Hi, I'm Alex Andrade

_Senior Design Engineer | Systems Architect | Community Builder_

I’m a Senior Design Engineer currently living in [Saskatoon, Canada - the land of living skies](https://www.instagram.com/visitsaskatoon/). Originally from Brazil, I moved here in 2019 to join the mission at [7shifts](https://www.7shifts.com/), where we build team management software that empower restaurant managers to reclaim their time.

![Me at the heart of Saskatoon](/assets/personal-image-2.png 'Me at the heart of Saskatoon')

### 🏗️ What I Do

I sit at the intersection of high-fidelity UI/UX and scalable frontend architecture. I don't just build features; I build the systems that make building features effortless.

- **Design Systems**: I am the founding architect of [Sous Chef](https://sous-chef.7shifts.software/), a comprehensive Design System that unified design and engineering at 7shifts.
- **Developer Experience (DX)**: I’m obsessed with creating pit of success workflows. If a tool or component isn't intuitive, accessible, and high-performance, it isn't finished.
- **Quality & Confidence**: I am a staunch advocate for the Testing Trophy. I believe tests should reflect how users interact with your code, not how you implemented it.

### 📈 My Journey

My career spans over **15 years**, taking me from the massive enterprise ERP landscapes of Latin America to the fast-paced world of Canadian startups.

- **The Enterprise Era**: Before Canada, I spent 4 years at [TOTVS](https://en.totvs.com/), the largest tech company in Latin America. There, I learned the discipline required to maintain massive software used by thousands of global customers.
- **The Startup Pivot**: At 7shifts and Living Skies Technologies, I transitioned into a frontend specialist, leading core migrations from legacy PHP to modern React SPAs and rescuing architectures from technical debt.
- **Today**: I now lead a dedicated **Design System team**, focusing on the future of UI—from semantic color tokens and dark mode to **AI-driven automation**.

## 🛠️ Developer Experience & Design Systems

In 2022, I joined a newly formed Developer Experience (DX) team at 7shifts with a clear mission: to remove friction from the frontend development lifecycle. My primary focus was the architecture and maturity of our internal Design System, [Sous Chef](https://sous-chef.7shifts.software/).

Sous Chef is the single source of truth for our UI/UX, transforming a fragmented set of components into a scalable, themeable ecosystem.

- **Foundational Architecture**: I led the planning and execution of the system from the ground up, establishing the core infrastructure using React, TypeScript, and Storybook.

- **Visual Confidence**: I integrated Chromatic into our CI/CD pipelines to automate visual regression testing, ensuring that every change across our 100+ components was intentional and bug-free.

- **Systemic Impact**: My work on the DX team was so foundational that it eventually led to the creation of a dedicated Design System team, where I now focus on high-fidelity craft and system evolution.
